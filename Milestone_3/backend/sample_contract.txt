MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

1. Payment Terms
Customer will pay all undisputed amounts within fifteen (15) days of invoice date.

2. Late Fees / Interest
Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month (or the maximum permitted by law, if lower).

3. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if such breach is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice.

4. Limitation of Liability
Each party’s aggregate liability is capped at the fees paid in the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim, except that liability for confidentiality breach is uncapped.

5. Service Levels (SLA)
Provider commits to 99.9% monthly uptime. Service credits apply if uptime falls below 99.9%.

6. Audit
Customer may audit Provider’s security controls annually upon reasonable notice.
